## ğŸ“Œ Problem Statement Background
Floods are among the most frequent and destructive disasters, causing loss of life, property damage, and disruption of essential services. Climate change, extreme rainfall, rapid urbanization, and poor drainage systems have increased flood risk in many regions. Current flood risk assessment methods often depend on manual GIS analysis and delayed reporting, making it difficult for authorities to identify high-risk zones early and plan preventive actions. With the availability of satellite imagery, rainfall records, and terrain data, an AI-based solution can help detect flood-prone areas faster and support timely decision-making for disaster preparedness and sustainable development.
